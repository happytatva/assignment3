About Golf Placement Services

Welcome to Golf Placement Services.com. Golf Placement Services.com is dedicated to helping students both gain the skills necessary to participate in college, as well as connect with schools that meet their academic, athletic, social and financial needs. Please take some time to navigate through the site to learn more about Brendan, his background in coaching and education and how you can put these superior skills to work for you!

In 2015, Golf Placement Services.com is pleased to announce that it will begin to support student athletes in the search of golf specific training academies in America. Golf Placement Services.com is uniquely equipped to fill this role having worked in collaboration with many different golf academies over the past 5 years. This gives Brendan unique insight into each academies culture, training programs, housing and support staff. Put this valuable knowledge to work for you by contacting Brendan and learning more about how he can help you!

Golf Placement Services.com is dedicated to the academic and athletic development of talented young men and women who wish to pursue golf scholarship opportunities. We specialize in helping students navigate the college search process and find opportunities to combine golf and education in a healthy, committed environment.
